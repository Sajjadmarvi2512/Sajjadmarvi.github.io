Hello, I'm Sajad

I'm a minimalist graphic designer with over 5 years of experience helping businesses grow through strategic design solutions. My approach combines aesthetic sensibility with business acumen to create designs that don't just look good—they drive results.

After studying Visual Communication Design, I worked with agencies before going independent to focus on meaningful collaborations with purpose-driven brands. I believe great design should be both beautiful and functional, solving real business challenges while creating memorable brand experiences.

My Design Philosophy

  • Less is more - clarity over clutter
  • Design should serve a strategic purpose
  • Every detail matters in brand perception
